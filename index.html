The First Artificial Intelligence: The Logic Theorist
December 9, 2022
This blog post discusses the first AI program in history, called the Logic Theorist. Developed by Allen Newell, Herbert Simon, and Cliff Shaw in 1955, this program was able to solve complex mathematical problems using logical reasoning, and it paved the way for many other AI programs in the following years. The success of the Logic Theorist showed that computers could solve complex problems using logical reasoning, and it sparked a great deal of interest in AI research and development. Today, AI is an essential part of many areas of science and technology, and it continues to advance the state of the art in AI research.
